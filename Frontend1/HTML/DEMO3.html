WE ARE PUZZLE


  Created and designed in Melbourne, we are a socially responsible bean to
cup artisan coffee house that offers specialty coffee with a focus on creating a
                         better world with coffee, one piece at a time.

Sourcing only the very best fairly traded speciality coffee beans from farmers
             around the world, a visit to Puzzle will be sure to complete your day.

Compostable, Biodegradable & Sustainable Non Paper Cups


Puzzle produces and only uses compostable and biodegradable coffee cups and straws that are 100% tree & plastic free and are made from plant pulp. Compost our cups to save the planet and help your garden grow!

We Plant A Tree For Every 100 Cups Of Coffee Sold


It'samazing to think that the fruit from a small tropical tree has become the way we wake up and get energised every morning. We play our part in the great coffee puzzle by donating 1 tree for every 100 cups of coffee and for each bag of coffee beans sold.

Roasted In Melbourne


Freshly roasted weekly in the world's coffee mecca of Melbourne, we are dedicated to always ensuring the highest quality Melbourne coffee no matter where you have your Puzzle Coffee worldwide.

We source the best fairly traded coffee beans from around the world direct to our stores.
